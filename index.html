Understanding the 3 Seller KPIs

Each KPI is a multiplier. Hit all 3 and you earn the full ₹1,400. Miss any, and your payout reduces proportionally.

A2I%
Appointment to Inspection Rate
Of all the appointment slots booked by your referred users, how many actually show up for the physical inspection at Cars24.
Baseline: 50%
💡 Improve this by sending high-intent users, setting appointment reminders, and filtering out casual inquiries.
I2SI%
Inspection to Stock-In Rate
Of all the cars inspected, how many does Cars24 actually purchase and add to their inventory. This measures lead quality.
Baseline: 16%
Elite Campaign: I2SI ≥ 3% → ₹700/inspection
💡 Improve by attracting genuine sellers with well-maintained cars, realistic price expectations, and proper documentation ready.
ASP
Average Selling Price
The average value of cars procured from your channel. Higher-value cars mean better ASP multiplier and a higher CPI.
Baseline: ₹2,75,000
💡 Target sellers with well-maintained or premium segment cars. 2019+ models and sedans/SUVs typically fetch higher ASP.

KPI Payout Conditions

For KPI-linked payouts (Seller & Buyer), performance directly determines your rate.

✅

All KPIs Achieved → Full Fixed Rate

When all KPIs meet or exceed baselines, you earn the maximum rate — ₹1,400/inspection (Seller) or ₹1,000/visit (Buyer). No reductions applied.

⚠️

One or More KPIs Missed → Pro-Rata Reduction

Missing any single KPI reduces your payout proportionally via the formula multiplier. Other achieved KPIs still contribute fully — only the missed metric's multiplier dips below 1×.

❌

All KPIs Missed → Significant Rate Drop

If all KPIs fall below baseline simultaneously, the combined multiplier effect compounds — causing a significant payout reduction. Focus on maintaining at least one KPI at baseline to protect your earnings.

Do's & Don'ts

These rules are part of your affiliate agreement. Violations can result in payout penalties or termination.

✅ Allowed Activities

  • SEO and organic content marketing
  • Social media campaigns (Meta, Instagram, YouTube)
  • Google Display and Performance Max ads (non-brand)
  • CRM and email marketing to your own opted-in list
  • WhatsApp business broadcasts (DND-compliant)
  • Programmatic display and retargeting
  • Influencer marketing (with prior approval)
  • Co-branded content with clear Cars24 attribution

❌ Prohibited Activities

  • Brand bidding on Cars24 keywords in paid search — will be penalised (terms in agreement)
  • DND (Do Not Disturb) calling or unsolicited messaging
  • Cookie stuffing or any form of affiliate fraud
  • Misleading claims about pricing, offers, or guarantees
  • Impersonating Cars24 brand, employees, or official pages
  • Spam tactics of any kind (bulk email, SMS blasting)
  • Fake or incentivised leads / click fraud
  • Sub-affiliating without prior written approval

Important Notice: Brand bidding will be explicitly penalised — detailed consequences are mentioned in your affiliate agreement. Any fraud detected will result in immediate payout hold and possible legal action.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum volume required to join the affiliate program?+
There is no fixed minimum volume requirement to join. However, Cars24 evaluates the quality and relevance of your traffic during the discussion phase (Phase 1). As long as your channel aligns with Cars24's target audience (car sellers/buyers), you can onboard regardless of size.
How is my payout calculated and when is it paid?+
Payouts are calculated at the end of each billing cycle using the applicable formula for each business stream. The cycle and exact payment terms are defined in your affiliate agreement. For Seller & Buyer payouts, KPI performance across the full month determines your effective rate. For VAS streams (PDI, Challan, VHR, LAC), it's a straightforward multiplication of volume × rate.
Can I run multiple business streams (Seller + Buyer + VAS) simultaneously?+
Yes — you can run all 6 revenue streams concurrently. Each stream has its own tracking, formula, and payout. Partners who diversify across multiple streams typically have higher total monthly earnings and more predictable income. Your agreement will define which verticals you're approved for.
How is my affiliate traffic tracked? What tech does Cars24 use?+
Cars24 uses Branch for App-based tracking and standard pixel/UTM tracking for Web. You'll receive unique affiliate links for each business vertical. Every action (lead, appointment, inspection, visit, order) is attributed to your ID in real-time. Tracking details are shared during Phase 1 and configured before go-live.
What happens if my A2I% or I2SI% drops below the baseline in a given month?+
Your CPI payout for that month is reduced proportionally. For example, if A2I% drops to 25% (50% of the 50% baseline), the A2I multiplier becomes 0.5× — reducing your CPI by 50% from that component alone. Other KPIs that remain at baseline continue to contribute their full 1× multiplier. The formula is multiplicative, so missing all three simultaneously compounds the reduction significantly.
Do I need a GST registration to become an affiliate partner?+
Not necessarily. Individual partners (freelancers, sole proprietors, or individuals without a registered company) can also work with Cars24 as affiliate partners. If you operate individually, a PAN card is the primary requirement. GST registration is required only if your annual earnings from affiliate commissions exceed the GST threshold (₹20L for services) or if you choose to register voluntarily. For registered businesses and companies, a valid GSTIN is mandatory for agreement execution. Either way, you can begin Phase 1 & 2 discussions immediately — the documentation is finalised in Phase 3.
What cities and geographies does the program cover?+
The program is PAN India, covering 182+ cities across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 markets. Cars24 operates 205+ branches. Campaign geography and city-specific KPI targets (if any) will be aligned during your Phase 1 discussion with the Cars24 team.

Partner Glossary
CPI — Cost Per Inspection
Money Cars24 pays you for every car seller you brought that got their car inspected. Max ₹1,400 per inspection.
100 sellers → 80 inspected → 80 × ₹1,400 = ₹1,12,000
CPV — Cost Per Visit
Money Cars24 pays you for every car buyer who visited a Cars24 hub. Max ₹1,000 per visit.
I2SI — Inspection to Stock-In
% of inspected cars Cars24 actually buys. Baseline 16%. Higher = better quality leads = higher payout.
A2I — Appointment to Inspection
% of appointments that actually show up for inspection. Baseline 50%.
V2D — Visit to Drive Ratio
% of hub visitors who do a test drive. Baseline 16%.
LAC — Loan Against Car
Car owner pledges their car as collateral but keeps driving it. You earn 1% of loan disbursed.
